Our Story

Simple n Spicy began with just one person, a craving for real flavor, and a kitchen filled with fiery aromas.

What started as a small batch of homemade pickle, crafted with love and curiosity, quickly turned into a mission — to bring authentic, bold taste back to every meal.

Using only fresh, local ingredients and a whole lot of spice, each jar tells the story of one person’s passion turned into a spicy movement.

No additives. No shortcuts. Just one simple truth — real taste is always spicy.
